# humanize — Strip AI writing patterns from text

> Detect and remove the telltale signs of AI-generated writing: inflated significance, em dash overuse, sycophantic tone, and AI vocabulary. Leaves natural human voice behind.

[![OpenClaw Skill](https://img.shields.io/badge/OpenClaw-Skill-blueviolet)](https://github.com/NachaFromMars)

## Overview
humanize scans text for AI writing patterns catalogued by Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" guide, rewrites the problematic sections with natural alternatives, and adds authentic voice. The goal is not just to remove bad patterns but to leave text that has personality — opinions, rhythm, specificity, first-person presence.

## Features
**Patterns detected:**
- Inflated significance — pivotal, testament, vital, crucial, underscores, indelible mark
- Media name-dropping — "featured in NYT/BBC/Wired"
- Promotional language
- Em dash overuse
- AI vocabulary (utilize, delve, leverage, etc.)
- Sycophantic tone

**Process:** scan → rewrite problematic sections → preserve meaning → add voice (opinions, rhythm variation, specificity, first person)

## Usage / Quick Start
Paste or point to the text you want humanized. Describe the intended tone if needed.
